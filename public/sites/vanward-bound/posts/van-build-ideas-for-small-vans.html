Van Build Ideas for Small Vans

Maximize space in small vans

Takeaway

Small vans can be converted into functional and comfortable living spaces

What happened

Small vans have become increasingly popular among van dwellers, offering a more fuel-efficient and maneuverable alternative to larger vans. However, building out a small van can be challenging, requiring creative solutions to maximize space and functionality. From clever storage solutions to multifunctional furniture, the right design can make a small van feel spacious and comfortable.

Why it matters

A well-designed van build can make a significant difference in the van life experience. It allows van dwellers to travel in comfort and style, even in smaller vehicles. With the right layout and features, small vans can be converted into functional and comfortable living spaces, perfect for weekend getaways or full-time van life.

How to think about it

When building out a small van, consider the essentials: bed, storage, and kitchenette. Look for space-saving solutions, such as murphy beds and foldable tables. Don't forget to invest in good insulation and ventilation to maintain a comfortable temperature and air quality.

Pros
  • Fuel efficiency
  • Maneuverability
  • Affordability
Cons
  • Limited space
  • Weight constraints
  • Design challenges
Watch out

Be cautious of overbuilding, which can compromise the van's weight and balance

FAQ

What are the best van build ideas for small vans?

Consider a murphy bed, foldable table, and compact kitchenette

How do I maximize storage in a small van?

Use vertical space, invest in storage bins, and utilize hidden compartments

Can I build out a small van myself?

Yes, but consider hiring a professional if you're not experienced with van builds
