What's Hot in Indoor Cycling

New gear for indoor cyclists

Takeaway

Upgrade your trainer for better performance

What happened

This year saw a surge in indoor cycling popularity. New trainers and platforms emerged, offering improved features and connectivity. Riders can now access a wide range of virtual routes and training programs.

Why it matters

With the rise of virtual racing, indoor cycling has become a key component of training. Having the right equipment and knowledge can significantly impact performance. Staying up-to-date with the latest trends and technology is crucial for competitive riders.

How to think about it

When choosing a trainer, consider factors such as power accuracy, compatibility, and noise level. Look for platforms that offer a variety of training programs and virtual routes to keep your workouts engaging and challenging.

Pros
  • Improved performance
  • Increased connectivity
  • Enhanced experience
Cons
  • Higher cost
  • Space requirements
  • Noise level
Watch out

Be cautious of cheap alternatives that may compromise on power accuracy and durability

FAQ

What is the best trainer for indoor cycling?

Look for a trainer with high power accuracy and connectivity options

How do I choose a virtual training platform?

Consider the variety of routes and training programs offered, as well as compatibility with your trainer

Can I use a smart trainer with a non-smart bike?

Yes, many smart trainers are compatible with non-smart bikes, but check the specifications before purchasing
